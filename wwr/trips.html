Half-Day Rapids Adventure

Experience the thrill in a short burst! Our half-day adventure is perfect for beginners and families seeking excitement without a long time commitment. Navigate Class II - III rapids with our expert guides while enjoying the lush riverside scenery.

Full-Day Canyon Expedition

Ready for a full day of adrenaline? The Canyon Expedition takes you through a series of thrilling Class III–IV rapids, with a scenic lunch break at a secluded beach. Great for adventurers looking to immerse themselves in nature and excitement.

Multi-Day Wild River Journey

Our ultimate rafting experience! Spend 2–3 days on the river with overnight camping, gourmet meals, and unforgettable stargazing. Paddle through Class IV rapids and explore remote natural beauty with expert safety and guidance.
